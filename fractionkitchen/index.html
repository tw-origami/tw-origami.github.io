Fraction Kitchen Guide

Each station turns a fraction skill into a kitchen job. The goal is not only to get the answer, but to see why the fraction makes sense in the recipe.

Kitchen Cards

Flip kitchen conversion cards like cups, tablespoons, teaspoons, ounces, pints, quarts, and gallons before you move into the recipe stations.

Scale Recipe

When servings change, multiply the ingredient by the same factor. Double the servings means double the fraction.

Measure It

Equivalent fractions can show the same amount. A cup filled to 6 out of 8 parts is the same as 3 out of 4 parts.

Fix the Batch

Wrong batches usually happen when someone keeps the original fraction or scales the wrong way. Compare the serving change first, then fix the amount.
