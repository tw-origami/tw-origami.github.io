Writing Sprint Guide

This app trains test-ready writing in short bursts. Some modes are multiple choice so you can practice quickly. Sprint mode lets you actually write, then compare your answer to a stronger model.

Sentence Combining

Look for the choice that sounds smooth, keeps all the meaning, and avoids weird repetition.

Fix the Paragraph

Think like an editor. Topic sentence, support, order, transitions, and complete sentences all matter.

CER Drill

A strong answer makes a claim, gives useful evidence, and explains why that evidence supports the claim.

3-Minute Sprint

Write fast, but not carelessly. Start with a clear point, add specific support, and finish with a sentence that sounds complete.
