Test Tactics Guide

These are the habits the app trains over and over. The goal is not only to know the answer, but to move through a test calmly and strategically.

Eliminate

Cross out answers that are impossible, too big, too small, or off topic before you do the full work.

Keyword Hunt

Underline words like in all, how many more, best evidence, and still on so you know what the question wants.

Pace It

Do not let one hard item eat the clock. Build a quick-win habit and come back later if time allows.

Bubble In

Keep the question number and answer sheet number matched, and check your line before moving on.

Skip & Return

Mark tough questions, move ahead, and return with fresh eyes if there is time at the end.

Decode It

Say the question in simpler words first. If you can explain the task clearly, the solving gets easier.
