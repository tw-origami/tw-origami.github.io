Reading Rescue Guide

Each passage comes with a question burst. After each answer, you can reveal highlighted evidence in the text so the app teaches the habit, not just the score.

Main Idea

Ask what the whole passage is mostly about, not which one detail sounds impressive.

Inference

Use clues from the text to figure out something the author suggests without stating directly.

Author's Purpose

Think about why the writer included a detail, sentence, or whole section.

Text Evidence

Pick the line that best proves an answer. Strong evidence is direct and specific.

Vocabulary in Context

Use nearby words and the situation in the passage to figure out what a word means there.

Timed Burst

The timer covers the whole passage burst, not one question at a time. Move steadily, skip panic, and keep reading with purpose.
